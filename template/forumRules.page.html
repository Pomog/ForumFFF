Forum Rules

Welcome to our awesome forum! Here are the rules that keep our digital realm a fun and friendly place:

  1. Respect Others: We’re all friends here! Be nice, like you would at a unicorn tea party. No grumpy dragons allowed!
  2. Stay On Topic: Let's stick to the quest at hand! Don’t wander off to realms unknown; the treasure's right here!
  3. No Promotions or Spam: Magic potions and enchanted brooms aside, no selling stuff or casting spam spells, please!
  4. Use Appropriate Language: Keep it PG-13! Save the epic battles for your D&D quests, and keep our kingdom squeaky clean.
  5. Respect Privacy: No peeking into others' crystal balls! Protect personal info like a wizard guards their spellbook.
  6. Follow Admin Instructions: If the forum sorcerer waves their wand, heed their magical commands! They're the real Gandalfs around here.

Remember, these rules are like spells that keep our community enchanting! Let's make this realm a place where trolls stay under bridges and joy roams free!
